Bellows Contracting LLC is a family-owned and operated cleaning company...

Bellows Contracting LLC is a family-owned and operated cleaning company proudly serving Ohio City and the surrounding areas. We specialize in both residential and commercial cleaning, delivering reliable, detail-oriented service with a personal touch. Whether it’s your home or business, we treat every space like it’s our own.

Why we do it

Jake and Megan Bellows

We do what we do because we believe everyone deserves to come home or walk into a workspace that feels clean, comfortable, and cared for. For us, cleaning isn’t just a task—it’s a way to make life a little easier for our clients and to bring peace of mind knowing their spaces are in good hands. As a family-owned business, we take pride in building trust and lasting relationships, and we approach every job with the same level of care and attention we would give our own home. Our goal is simple: to deliver dependable, high-quality service that makes a real difference in the day-to-day lives of the people we serve.
